Basic Air Fryer Hot Dogs Recipe

Description

Perfectly crips hot dogs and toasted buns made in minutes with the help of your air fryer. Top with ketchup, mustard, relish, chili, or condiments of choice.

Prep: 5 mins
cook: 5 mins
total: 10 mins
Servings: 4
Yield: 4 servings
Nutrition Info

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place buns in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 2 minutes. Remove buns to a plate.
  3. Place hot dogs in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 3 minutes. Transfer hot dogs to buns.
